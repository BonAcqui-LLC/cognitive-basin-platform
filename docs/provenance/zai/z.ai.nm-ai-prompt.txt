# The Bigger Fish

The immediate product target is not Motorola activation.

Motorola or phone activation is a later embodiment and onboarding path.

The immediate target is not merely a web platform either.

The current engineering target is:

A local developmental runtime that can:

1. receive a bounded task environment;
2. act through explicit local rules;
3. spend finite resources;
4. record consequences of its own actions;
5. alter later choices from prior experience;
6. preserve failures as usable constraints;
7. form persistent associations across repeated encounters;
8. recover from contradiction or failed routing;
9. explain its behavior from recorded internal state;
10. remain inspectable, replayable, and provenance-bearing.

Phone, activation, UI, cloud, and product packaging come after the developmental core is demonstrated.

## Stage 4 — Local failure/scar memory

Only after Stage 3 review.

Test whether failed or blocked local actions leave temporary negative constraints.

Do not hard-code dead-end escape unless the trail-plus-scar system fails to produce recovery.

## Stage 5 — Minimal Cognitive Basin

Add persistent association above Natural Math rather than hiding it inside Natural Math.

Begin with:

* encounter or brush records
* local outcome history
* attractor strength
* contradiction scars
* recovery routes
* retrieval frequency
* survival weighting
* pruning
* replay

The basin must preserve provenance back to the experiences that formed it.

Do not begin with language generation.

## Stage 6 — Fractalish runtime integration

Map the developing system through:

PERCEPT
→ ATAL
→ RIGOR
→ CIRCUIT
→ GUARD
→ SERA

Initial meanings:

* PERCEPT: receive and normalize bounded observations
* ATAL: assign pressure, salience, deficit, and regulatory state
* RIGOR: challenge candidate interpretations and expose contradiction
* CIRCUIT: maintain recursive processing and active continuity
* GUARD: constrain unsafe, incoherent, or disallowed action
* SERA: preserve cost, provenance, retention, replay, and audit

## Stage 7 — Construction A+, PEFP, and SymLan

Do not treat these as built-in AutoClaw features.

Inventory their authoritative documents and code now, but implement them later.

Use this relationship:

* Natural Math creates bounded processes and histories.
* MCVA describes stable morphology and process configurations.
* Construction A+ / Honeybee / PiA+ reads sufficiently settled attractor structure into discrete glyph candidates.
* PEFP tests persistence and recurrence of attractor expression where applicable.
* SymLan represents only states and vocabularies that have earned stability and replay support.
* Unsettled states remain unresolved rather than being forced into symbols.

Construction A+ and SymLan must not be omitted from the architecture, but they must not be prematurely substituted for learning or understanding.

## Stage 8 — Developmental task ladder

Test progressively:

1. move toward a resource;
2. retain successful local action;
3. avoid repeated local failure;
4. recover from blockage;
5. adapt when the resource moves;
6. choose between competing resources;
7. trade danger against reward;
8. preserve group energy while seeking;
9. recognize repeated environment patterns;
10. report why an action occurred using its state and provenance history.

Behavior comes first.
Explanation must be grounded in recorded state rather than invented afterward.
