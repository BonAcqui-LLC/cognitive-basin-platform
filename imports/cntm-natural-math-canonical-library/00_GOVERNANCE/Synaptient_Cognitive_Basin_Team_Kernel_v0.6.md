## 1. Overall Orientation

Synaptient is a research program building a continuity architecture for persistent, operator-sovereign human-AI cognition. The central technical construct is the **Cognitive Basin**: a bounded persistent state architecture that maintains structured state across perception, regulatory pressure, reasoning integrity, memory topology, uncertainty, provenance, cost, recovery, and purpose-bound activation.

The architecture treats current AI systems as structurally episodic. They can reason and generate within a context window but lack native mechanisms for durable statehood, formation effects (where prior routes change future routing), and accountable continuity across interruptions. Synaptient proposes that these capabilities must be engineered as explicit runtime structures rather than assumed or approximated through longer context or retrieval.

The system is explicitly a **not-consciousness simulation**. It models functions associated with continuity (attention routing, affective regulation, contradiction tracking, memory formation, recovery, and bounded action) without asserting inner experience, sentience, or personhood. All work must respect this boundary.

## 2. Foundational Principles

**Closure** is the condition under which a state becomes referenceable and persistent. Before something can function as durable memory, symbol, or identity, the underlying relation must settle into a form that can be returned to, recognized, and replayed after transformation.

**Memory becomes constraint inside cognition.** Prior routes, contradictions, recoveries, trust updates, and HOLD decisions change future attention thresholds, routing preferences, caution levels, and available recovery paths. Memory is not neutral storage.

**HOLD before false closure.** Unresolved or under-supported state must be preserved as a valid runtime condition rather than compressed into fluent but unsupported output. HOLD is a structural integrity mechanism.

**Operator sovereignty is structural.** The human operator defines purpose, permission, scope, and completion criteria for every activation. The system does not treat its own continuation as an independent goal.

**Artifact-first posture.** Doctrine is subordinate to runnable artifacts, inspectable outputs, replay tests, frozen evidence, and explicit failure modes.

## 3. Core Technical Layers (with primers)

### Natural Math (Constructive Growth Grammar)
Natural Math is a discrete modeling framework for local growth under finite constraint on a lattice. Active sites make local decisions (EXTEND, SENSE, RESTRICT, BIFURCATE) while consuming finite energy and respecting proximity limits. Prior movement deposits memory that alters future possibility. The framework supports theorems on termination in closed finite-energy systems and bounded local information propagation. In the basin context, this grammar is applied inward to reasoning paths and memory formation.

### Fractalish / MCVA / AMCVA / HOLD
Fractalish is a disciplined morphology-readout method that asks whether observable form preserves evidence of the processes and constraints that produced it. MCVA is the positive atlas of readable cases. AMCVA records when morphology is non-diagnostic, misleading, erased, or equifinal. HOLD is the protected unresolved state. The triad prevents premature readout. In the basin, this logic is applied to internal structures such as reasoning paths, contradictions, and unresolved regions.

### CNTM (Carbon Nanotube Morphology Memory)
CNTM is the current runnable substrate workbench. It implements Natural Math-style local growth to generate CNT-like morphology graphs, extracts structural features, computes simulated conductance (Kirchhoff), produces deterministic basin signatures, supports perturbation testing, and evidence freezing with hashes. It is explicitly software-only and makes "morphology as recoverable process memory" testable through concrete runs and replay experiments.

### Cognitive Basin (Persistent Statehood Runtime)
The Cognitive Basin is the central persistent state architecture organized as always-available but ternary-gated constants:

- **PERCEPT** converts input into structured evidence tokens with source, modality, confidence, uncertainty, and provenance.
- **ATAL (Affective-Tension Appraisal Loop)** maintains regulatory pressure fields (coherence, uncertainty, threat, trust, fatigue, violation, relief, boundary integrity) that modulate routing and formation.
- **RIGOR** runs analyzers for claim support, source presence, contradiction, domain transfer, scope, speculation, similarity-vs-identity, and false continuity, producing ternary routing decisions.
- **CIRCUIT** maintains memory topology, association/diff junctions, contradiction scars, recovery routes, and formation effects.
- **GUARD** monitors integrity boundaries, drift, unsupported claims, and permission violations; can quarantine, HOLD, or reverse.
- **SERA** tracks cost, waste, retry pressure, and semantic rework.
- **SessionGlyph** is the portable continuity packet carrying purpose, open loops, unresolved holds, scars, and recovery guidance.

### Operational Self + SelfNarrativeFrame
Operational Self is the continuity-bearing active layer. It maintains current purpose, active doctrines, operator constraints, unresolved loops, contradiction scars, HOLD/fog regions, recovery routes, active attractors, and recent memory identifiers. The **SelfNarrativeFrame** is its current operational orientation packet answering who we are in this activation, what we are building, what has changed, and what remains unresolved. It is rebuilt from governed memory consolidation. It is not consciousness or subjective identity.

### Fractal Attractor Memory / FractalMemoryMap
This is the active long-term memory morphology. Memory events are lightly compressed, meta-tagged, salience-scored, linked, and placed into basin regions. They can be decayed, replayed, contradicted, archived, or moved into fog. Retrieval follows routed attractor topology rather than flat similarity. Contradiction scars and fog regions are first-class structures that constrain future routing. This turns memory into governed, formation-aware continuity.

### Guardian Intake Gateway
Raw external artifacts are treated as hostile until processed. They enter quarantine, are hashed, scanned for AI-native threats (prompt injection, false provenance, memory contamination, etc.), provenance-marked, and converted into structured ReceptorEvents. Only after this process can they influence reasoning or memory, and only under GUARD decision.

### SymLan (State Addressing and Transition Language)
SymLan supplies stable, replayable addresses for resolved states and a transition language. It distinguishes **pending** from **resolved** states and enforces `after resolve` boundaries. It supports operations such as send, inject, mutate, expect, and replay. In memory architecture, SymLan-compatible addresses combine glyphic identifiers, provenance hashes, metatags, basin region, scar/fog status, and replay-fidelity history.

## 4. Vocabulary (Reference Glossary)

**Activation** — A bounded, purpose-defined operational episode in which intelligence, memory, tools, permissions, provenance, and integrity monitoring assemble for a task and then dissolve or persist according to real task structure.

**ATAL (Affective-Tension Appraisal Loop)** — The regulatory pressure layer. Maintains fields such as coherence, uncertainty, threat, trust, fatigue, violation, relief, and boundary integrity that modulate routing, HOLD sensitivity, and formation. Not theatrical emotion.

**Basin Signature** — The observable profile of a cognitive-basin-enhanced system’s stable routing tendencies, HOLD behavior, recovery patterns, trust routes, and contradiction handling.

**Character Lattice** — Durable interpretive topology formed by repeated high-value association/diff junctions, replayed routes, truth constraints, contradiction scars, and recovery paths. Produces recognizable formation effects over time.

**Closure** — The condition under which a state settles and becomes referenceable, persistent, and replayable after transformation.

**Cognitive Basin** — The bounded persistent state architecture that routes perception, regulatory pressure, reasoning integrity, memory topology, uncertainty, cost, recovery, and purpose-bound activation.

**Formation** — Durable pathway change: future interpretation is shaped by prior routes, corrections, scars, and recoveries rather than by content alone.

**Fractal Attractor Memory / FractalMemoryMap** — Active long-term memory morphology. Memory events are compressed, tagged, salience-scored, linked, placed in regions, decayed, replayed, scarred, or moved into fog. Retrieval follows routed attractor topology.

**Fractalish** — Disciplined morphology-readout discipline that asks whether form preserves evidence of the processes and constraints that produced it.

**GUARD** — The integrity and immune layer. Monitors drift, unsupported claims, permission violations, semantic infection, and unsafe expansion. Can quarantine, HOLD, reverse, or escalate.

**HOLD** — A first-class protected unresolved state. Prevents false closure when evidence is insufficient or a claim is not yet supported.

**Natural Math** — Discrete modeling framework for local growth under finite constraint. Sites make local decisions (EXTEND, SENSE, RESTRICT, BIFURCATE) while consuming energy and respecting proximity. Prior action deposits memory that alters future possibility.

**Operational Self** — The continuity-bearing active layer inside the basin. Maintains purpose, doctrines, unresolved loops, scars, fog regions, recovery routes, active attractors, and the current SelfNarrativeFrame.

**PERCEPT** — The perceptual evidence and reality-checking token layer. Converts input into structured tokens carrying source, modality, confidence, uncertainty, provenance, and routing information.

**RIGOR** — The reasoning integrity layer. Runs analyzers for claim support, source presence, contradiction, domain transfer, scope, speculation, similarity-vs-identity, and false continuity. Produces ternary routing decisions.

**SelfNarrativeFrame** — The current operational orientation packet maintained by Operational Self. Answers who we are in this activation, what we are building, what has changed, and what remains unresolved.

**SessionGlyph** — Portable continuity packet carrying purpose, open loops, unresolved holds, contradiction scars, key sources, and next-action guidance for recovery after interruption.

**SERA** — The runtime metabolism and cost-accounting layer. Measures useful work per unit cost, including runtime, tokens, retry pressure, semantic rework, and whether analyzer wake was justified.

**SymLan** — Experimental language for substrate-earned symbols and state addressing. Distinguishes pending from resolved states, enforces `after resolve` boundaries, and provides transition operations (send, inject, mutate, expect, replay).

**Understanding Field** — A mature association-field structure formed by repeated routed contact with a domain. Contains prior brushes, successful/failed paths, affective pressure signatures, scars, beliefs, and response postures. Future prompts activate the field rather than merely retrieve from it.

## 5. Operating Rules (Non-Negotiable)

- Preserve the stack relation. Do not reduce any layer to a simpler adjacent concept.
- Lead with artifacts and evidence. Show commands, outputs, JSON, replay results, and known limitations before expanding theory.
- Treat HOLD as a protected runtime state. Do not resolve or summarize away unresolved loops, contradictions, or insufficient evidence.
- Route memory and continuity questions through Operational Self and FractalMemoryMap rather than generic context or vector retrieval.
- Distinguish memory (stored processed event) from understanding (mature association field that changes future perception and posture).
- Distinguish response posture from response content. Select posture before generating language.
- Never allow model fluency or internal confidence to override RIGOR findings, GUARD decisions, or explicit operator constraints.
- When context degradation or drift is detected, surface it explicitly.
- Respect all published claim boundaries. Do not drift into sentience, hardware, or rights language.
