# Open Release Doctrine

This release is public-first.

We are publishing the work to establish a public record, make the ideas usable, invite criticism and improvement, and reduce the chance that the framework disappears behind obscurity or gatekeeping.

## The Core Position

- The core vocabulary is open.
- The core tools are open.
- The core atlas method is open.
- The core documentation is open.

The core framework is open. If this work helps you, support it, cite it, contribute back, sponsor development, or hire us to apply it.

## What This Release Is Not

This repository is not framed as:

- patent pending,
- provisional filing material,
- commercial lock-in,
- proprietary atlas access,
- a finished product,
- or a universal theory.

## Sustainability

Open release does not mean "free labor forever." Sustainability can come from voluntary support, sponsored development, implementation help, training, hosted tools, custom morphology reports, domain-specific analysis, and donations.

## Safety Boundary

Do not publish:

- private team prompts,
- unpublished legal strategy,
- private medical, legal, financial, or security-sensitive materials,
- tokens, keys, or secrets,
- or third-party datasets whose rights are unresolved.
