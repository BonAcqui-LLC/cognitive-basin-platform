Docs / Fractal Math Primer

Useful anchors, not the whole story.

Fractal dimension measures scaling complexity. It does not identify morphology by itself.

Canonical families such as Koch, Sierpinski, Hilbert, dragon, branching trees, and Mandelbrot-like boundaries help define the alphabet. Physical morphology becomes the language.

Fractalish therefore uses fractal dimension alongside topology, lacunarity, anisotropy, symmetry, periodicity, branch structure, and SVG trace geometry.
