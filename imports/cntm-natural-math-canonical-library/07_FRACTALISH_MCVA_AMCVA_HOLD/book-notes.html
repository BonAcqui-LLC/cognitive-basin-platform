Book Notes

The book notes are the long trail behind the framework.

This is the place for working notes on the Fractalish series: discovery story, vocabulary formation, Morphological Memory Theory, MCVA and AMCVA, negative regimes, machine-native vision, and the recurring effort to make a strange but useful worldview readable without letting it turn into overclaim.

For newcomers, this section matters because it preserves the longer continuity of the work. Not everyone will enter through code or descriptors. Some people will need the narrative and conceptual trail to understand why the stack looks the way it does.

Some of these notes will become essays, some will become chapters, some will become diagrams, and some will remain fragments that are still doing conceptual work behind the scenes.

Publicly, this section should feel like a real notebook rather than marketing copy: grounded, incomplete, and generous about what is still being figured out.
