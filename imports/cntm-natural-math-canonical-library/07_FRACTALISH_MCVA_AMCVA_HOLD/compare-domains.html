Does the language travel?

Cross-domain comparison tests whether recurring morphology families remain comparable without pretending they are identical.

The MVP comparison bench focuses on primary and secondary family matches, shared and divergent descriptors, deltas for fractal dimension, lacunarity, branching, endpoints, junctions, density, anisotropy, curvature, and side-by-side SVG traces.

A circle can constrain a branch. A lattice can guide a dendrite. A wave can carry a fracture.
