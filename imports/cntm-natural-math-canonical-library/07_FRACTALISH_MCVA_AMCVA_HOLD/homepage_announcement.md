# Homepage Announcement

Use this as the short launch statement on Fractalish or at the top of the public repository.

## Version A

Fractalish and MCVA are now being released in the open.

This is a public research framework for reading process from form: MCVA, AMCVA, HOLD, Natural Math, Morphological Memory Theory, SERA, and the first atlas and evidence-building lanes.

We are not putting the core framework behind a patent wall or proprietary gate. We are publishing it so people can read it, test it, criticize it, improve it, cite it, and contribute back.

This is not a finished product, not a diagnostic engine, and not a universal theory. It is an open research stack with a clear first lane: morphology as process evidence, starting in public-safe domains such as roads, fractures, and other visible morphology-bearing structures.

The core framework is open. If this work helps you, support it, cite it, sponsor development, or hire us to apply it.

## Version B

We are releasing the core Fractalish / MCVA framework openly.

What is public now:

- the whitepaper and doctrine,
- the MCVA / AMCVA / HOLD framing,
- Natural Math as the generative layer,
- SERA as the cost and quality layer,
- the first atlas and evidence-library structure,
- and the first public application lane around roads and public works.

What this is not:

- not a medical diagnostic system,
- not a disaster predictor,
- not a universal law,
- and not a finished product.

What it is:

An open research framework for reading process from form, preserving uncertainty, and comparing before claiming.
