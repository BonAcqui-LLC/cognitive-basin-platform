# Project Map

## Fractalish

Public doorway and human-facing explanation for physical, fractal-like, and morphology-bearing forms.

## MCVA

Morphological Computation Vocabulary Atlas. The open vocabulary and readout framework for morphology-bearing process evidence.

## AMCVA

The guardrail and negative atlas for absent, erased, hidden, misleading, or insufficient morphology.

## HOLD

The unresolved evidentiary state. HOLD means do not force a binary answer.

## Natural Math

The forward or generative local-process simulation layer. Finite local updates, bounded sensing, dynamic limits, process states like `EXTEND` / `SENSE` / `RESTRICT`, memory fields, and halt conditions.

## Morphological Memory Theory

The thesis that morphology may preserve process evidence, while preserving equifinality and uncertainty.

## SERA

Efficiency and resilience accounting: cost, waste, retries, false commitments, HOLD rate, and valid output.

## Infinitysight

Future machine-native perception layer for extracting morphology-bearing signals across sensors and scales.

## Construction A+

Future morphology-token and readout architecture. It is not identical to Natural Math.

## SymLan

Future symbolic compression layer for stable, validated morphology and process states.

## T81 / Axion

Future deterministic runtime, provenance, and HOLD-mapping layer.

## MCVA Atlas

Open manifest and baseline library of morphology examples, descriptors, source and license metadata, and AMCVA boundary cases.
