Commons / Submit

Submit a case with its trace and metadata intact.

The system should accept proof-positive, proof-negative, and HOLD cases.

Required submission metadata includes title, domain, subdomain, source, uploader, image type, timepoint count, timestamp quality, tracked-object status, known outcome, license status, morphology class, routing, notes, limitations, and consent/privacy confirmation.

Never silently overwrite submitted records.

Proof-Positive

Readable morphology with adequate segmentation and descriptor quality.

Proof-Negative

Absent, obscured, erased, or geometry-dominant counterexamples.

HOLD

Ambiguous, weakly segmented, or domain-mismatched cases that need restraint.
