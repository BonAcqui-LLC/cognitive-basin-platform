Project Map

How the stack fits together.

Fractalish is the public doorway and human-facing explanation layer for physical, fractal-like, and morphology-bearing forms.

  • MCVA: the open vocabulary and readout framework for morphology-bearing process evidence.
  • AMCVA: the guardrail and negative atlas for absent, erased, hidden, misleading, or insufficient morphology.
  • HOLD: the unresolved evidentiary state. Do not force a binary answer.
  • Natural Math: the forward or generative local-process simulation layer.
  • Morphological Memory Theory: the thesis that morphology may preserve process evidence while preserving uncertainty.
  • SERA: efficiency and resilience accounting for cost, waste, retries, false commitments, HOLD rate, and valid output.
  • Infinitysight: future machine-native perception layer.
  • Construction A+: future morphology-token and readout architecture; not identical to Natural Math.
  • SymLan: future symbolic compression layer for stable, validated morphology and process states.
  • T81 / Axion: future deterministic runtime, provenance, and HOLD-mapping layer.
  • MCVA Atlas: open manifest and baseline library of examples, descriptors, metadata, and AMCVA boundary cases.

The main split to remember is simple: Natural Math is the generative layer. MCVA is the readout layer.
