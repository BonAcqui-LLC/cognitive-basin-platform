# SERA Metrics

SERA is the accounting layer for cost, waste, retries, false commitments, HOLD rate, and valid output.

## Why SERA Exists

Accuracy alone hides important workflow costs. A system can appear accurate while still wasting review effort, forcing premature commitments, or generating many retries.

## Early v0.1-alpha Metrics

Initial public metrics may include:

- `hold_rate`
- `coverage`
- `false_positive_commitments_avoided`
- `valid_committed_classification_rate`
- `review_load_rate`
- `retry_pressure`

These are early research metrics, not certified enterprise KPIs.

## First Application Lane

The first application lane should be roads and public works:

**SERA for roads: morphology-based road quality assessment and maintenance prioritization.**

That should be framed as a proposed open protocol, not a certified standard.
