Fractalish must read the whole geometric field.

Nature does not speak only in fractals.

Fractalish does not claim that every natural pattern is fractal. Nature uses many geometries. Fractalish studies when morphology preserves process information, and AMCVA marks when that information is absent, hidden, erased, dominated by another geometry, or not yet readable.

MCVA reads preserved process. AMCVA prevents hallucinated process.
