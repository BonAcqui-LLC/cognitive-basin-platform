# MCVA / AMCVA Overview

## MCVA

MCVA is the positive readout lane. It asks when observed morphology contains enough structure to be measured, baselined, and compared as process evidence.

## AMCVA

AMCVA is the negative guardrail atlas. It covers absent, erased, hidden, blurred, contaminated, misleading, low-resolution, or otherwise non-diagnostic morphology.

## Why the Split Matters

Without AMCVA and HOLD, a morphology-first framework drifts toward over-reading. The point is not to say every branching or fracture pattern means the same thing. The point is to preserve a disciplined comparison workflow when morphology is present and preserve restraint when it is not.
