## 1. Open Release Doctrine

We are not filing a provisional patent for MCVA/AMCVA today.

We are releasing the core work openly to establish a public record, invite use, accelerate criticism and improvement, and prevent the work from being trapped behind gatekeeping, credentials, or institutional permission.

The open-release doctrine is:

> The core vocabulary is open.  
> The core code is open.  
> The core atlas method is open.  
> The core documentation is open.  
> If this work helps you, cite it, fork it, improve it, sponsor it, or hire us to apply it.

This is the honor-system roadside stand model. Take what helps. Leave something if you can. Improve it if you are able.

This release is not anti-sustainability. It allows support, sponsorship, paid implementation, custom morphology reports, domain-specific analysis, hosted tools, teaching, training, grants, and partnerships. It simply rejects locking the core framework behind patents or proprietary access as the first move.

### Public release goals

1. Establish a clear timestamped public record.
2. Make the framework usable by researchers, engineers, independent builders, students, and domain experts.
3. Define MCVA/AMCVA/HOLD in a way others can test.
4. Publish initial code and docs with permissive open licenses.
5. Preserve uncertainty rather than overclaim.
6. Separate public-safe work from private, medical, legal, financial, or unlicensed material.
7. Make Fractalish.com the public doorway.
8. Make GitHub the working public lab notebook.

### Public release licensing

Use:

- **Third-party datasets/images:** do not include unless their license is verified. If useful, list them by manifest with source URL and license status.

## 2. Short public thesis

**MCVA** stands for **Morphological Computation Vocabulary Atlas**.

MCVA is an open research framework for measuring morphology-bearing structures as process evidence. It combines morphology descriptors, ternary MCVA/HOLD/AMCVA decision gating, provenance records, Natural Math generative simulations, and SERA efficiency metrics for comparing observed morphology to candidate process histories.

MCVA is not a medical diagnostic system, not a universal theory, and not a replacement for domain experts. It is a software-first toolkit for building reproducible morphology baselines, testing process-memory hypotheses, and preserving uncertainty through a first-class HOLD state.

Core phrase:

> **Read form carefully. Preserve uncertainty. Compare before claiming.**

Expanded thesis:

> When morphology-bearing structures repeatedly appear within a domain, they should be preserved, measured, baselined, compared, and monitored as potential signal rather than dismissed as visual coincidence, generic efficiency, or decorative fractal similarity. MCVA studies those morphology snapshots. AMCVA guards against over-reading when morphology is absent, erased, hidden, misleading, or non-diagnostic. HOLD preserves uncertainty when the evidence is insufficient.

## 3. What we are and are not claiming

### We are not claiming

MCVA/AMCVA does not claim:

- a new law of physics;
- a universal theory of fractals;
- a theory of everything;
- that all branching systems are identical;
- that all morphology preserves recoverable process information;
- that all fractals mean the same thing;
- that morphology uniquely identifies cause;
- that MCVA diagnoses disease;
- that MCVA predicts heart attacks, strokes, embolisms, miscarriages, tumors, earthquakes, bridge failures, sinkholes, avalanches, road collapses, or disasters;
- that MCVA replaces clinicians, engineers, inspectors, physicists, material scientists, mathematicians, or domain experts;
- that Natural Math replaces existing mathematics, physics, biology, or engineering models;
- that Infinitysight, Construction A+, or SymLan are complete products in this public release;
- that included code is regulatory-certified.

### We are claiming a narrower lane

MCVA/AMCVA does claim a practical open research lane:

- Some morphology-bearing structures may preserve measurable evidence about process, constraint, stress, flow, growth, failure, memory, or boundary history.
- Many domains already observe these forms, but often lack a shared cross-domain vocabulary and comparison workflow.
- A ternary gate is safer than forced binary classification when morphology is ambiguous.
- A negative/guardrail atlas is necessary to prevent over-reading.
- Observed morphology can be compared to baseline atlases and to generated/simulated morphology.
- Reproducible descriptors, provenance records, and HOLD states can make morphology work more testable.
- Cost, waste, false commitment, retry, and efficiency can be measured with SERA.

### Core working sentence

> **Morphology records process — except when it does not, and that is why AMCVA and HOLD exist.**

## 4. MCVA

**MCVA** means **Morphological Computation Vocabulary Atlas**.

MCVA is the positive/readout side of the framework. It identifies morphology-bearing structures that contain enough measurable organization to support descriptor extraction, baseline comparison, process-hypothesis scoring, or domain/subdomain classification.

An MCVA-positive morphology is not merely visually attractive or “fractal-looking.” It is morphology that can be treated as a structured evidence object.

Examples of MCVA-bearing morphology may include:

- road cracks;
- alligator pavement cracking;
- bridge crack networks;
- fracture surfaces;
- dendritic solidification;
- battery dendrites;
- vascular branching;
- retinal vasculature;
- root and mycelium networks;
- river deltas and drainage networks;
- Lichtenberg figures;
- lightning scars and electrical discharge paths;
- semiconductor/lithography defects;
- plume, eddy, and vortex structures;
- erosion fronts;
- tumor angiogenesis as research-only / no diagnosis;
- hemodynamic or neurophysiological morphology/time-series as research-only / no diagnosis.

MCVA must operate by domain. A river delta, retinal vessel map, battery dendrite, and road crack may share branching or network-like morphology, but they do not mean the same thing. MCVA compares within domains first, adjacent domains second, and cross-domain only after repeatable descriptor patterns emerge.

## 5. AMCVA

**AMCVA** means the anti-MCVA / negative guardrail atlas.

AMCVA is not simply “bad morphology.” It is the explicit classification space for cases where morphology is:

- absent;
- erased;
- hidden;
- occluded;
- blurred;
- misleading;
- non-diagnostic;
- contaminated;
- insufficiently resolved;
- outside known domain;
- too ambiguous to interpret;
- not morphology-bearing enough;
- produced by a process not represented in the atlas.

AMCVA prevents MCVA from becoming a “fractals everywhere” overclaim.

AMCVA is essential because some domains leave strong process traces, some leave weak traces, some traces are erased, and some visual similarities are false friends.

AMCVA examples:

- a road image with shadows that mimic cracks;
- a medical image with insufficient resolution for vessel analysis;
- a SEM patch with metadata confounds;
- a morphology that looks branching but has no reliable domain baseline;
- a degraded or compressed image where descriptor output cannot be trusted;
- a structure whose process history is not recoverable from available evidence.

AMCVA can route to:
- no decision;
- need better data;
- need segmentation;
- need higher resolution;
- need domain expert review;
- need different modality;
- not morphology-bearing;
- not diagnostic;
- misleading visual analogy.

## 6. HOLD

**HOLD** is a first-class unresolved evidentiary state.

HOLD is not a failure. HOLD is the system refusing premature commitment.

HOLD means:

> The system lacks sufficient evidence to commit to MCVA or AMCVA.

HOLD should be emitted when:

- confidence is low;
- evidence is conflicting;
- resolution is insufficient;
- segmentation is poor;
- the sample may be out-of-distribution;
- the domain/subdomain is unknown;
- baseline coverage is inadequate;
- the morphology may support multiple process hypotheses;
- medical/regulatory/engineering thresholds are not met;
- the system needs more sensing, human review, or better metadata.

HOLD is distinct from:

- **AMCVA:** absent/non-diagnostic/misleading morphology.
- **Quarantine:** unsafe, contaminated, or security-relevant isolation.
- **Deny/Reject:** affirmative block.
- **Warn:** proceed with caution but not unresolved.
- **Defer:** postpone action, not necessarily evidence uncertainty.

HOLD is the anti-drift state. It prevents forced binary decision-making from pretending uncertainty has been resolved.

## 7. MCVA Gate

**MCVA Gate** is the ternary decision layer:

> **MCVA / HOLD / AMCVA**

The gate receives descriptors, metadata, baseline comparisons, confidence scores, and optionally Natural Math process hypotheses. It emits a decision record.

A minimal MCVA Gate should include:

- descriptor extraction;
- confidence scoring;
- hold band;
- MCVA/HOLD/AMCVA decision;
- hold reason;
- recommended action;
- provenance hash;
- canonical JSON output;
- optional chaining to previous decision ID;
- SERA metrics.

Example output:

```json
{
  "schema": "mcva.decision.v0",
  "created_utc": "2026-06-05T00:00:00Z",
  "input_path": "examples/synthetic_demo/sample_001.png",
  "domain": "synthetic",
  "subdomain": "branching_trace",
  "source_sha256": "...",
  "descriptor_version": "mcva.descriptors.v0",
  "descriptors": {
    "foreground_density": 0.182,
    "roughness_proxy": 0.431,
    "component_count": 3
  },
  "decision": "HOLD",
  "confidence": 0.52,
  "hold_reason": "low_confidence",
  "recommended_action": "request_more_data_or_human_review",
  "decision_id": "..."
}
```

Initial hold reasons:

- low_confidence;
- insufficient_resolution;
- segmentation_quality_unknown;
- feature_conflict;
- out_of_distribution_unknown;
- baseline_missing;
- ambiguous_process_hypotheses;
- regulatory_threshold_not_met.

## 8. Morphological Memory Theory

**Morphological Memory Theory** is the thesis that morphology may preserve recoverable information about generating processes, constraints, flows, stresses, boundaries, failures, reinforcements, and prior states.

Public-safe phrasing:

> The final form of a morphology-bearing system may contain measurable evidence of the local constraints and process history that produced it.

Important caveat:

> Equifinality matters. Different processes can produce similar morphology. MCVA must not pretend morphology uniquely identifies cause.

Morphological Memory Theory asks:

- What process generated this form?
- What constraints shaped it?
- What state information might it preserve?
- What is normal for this domain/subdomain?
- What is abnormal or drifting?
- What precursor changes appear before transition or failure?
- What descriptor best captures the difference?
- When should the system emit HOLD?

Core phrase:

> **Memory becomes geometry.**

Expanded:

> Trails become routes. Pressure becomes branching. Failure becomes boundary. Success becomes channel. Prior form becomes future constraint.

## 9. Field-forensics model

MCVA researchers should be trained like field naturalists, forensic inspectors, and practical domain experts, not merely image classifiers.

Field forensics reads ordinary visible features as evidence of prior process. For example, a forest expert may read stone walls, tree scars, pits, mounds, windthrow direction, limb whorls, smoothness of ground, and boundary artifacts as evidence of prior land use, storms, agricultural history, and disturbance.

MCVA should read morphology similarly:

- observe the form;
- preserve the evidence;
- classify domain/subdomain;
- identify process markers;
- compare to known baselines;
- infer history cautiously;
- route uncertainty to HOLD;
- document the reasoning path.

Core training question:

> What happened here, what else could make this shape, what baseline applies, and what evidence would falsify this interpretation?

## 10. Natural Math

**Natural Math** is the forward/generative process layer.

It is not a new mathematics.
It is not a replacement for physics.
It is not a theory of everything.

Best cautious definition:

> Natural Math is a finite local-process modeling framework for systems that grow, branch, reroute, stabilize, or halt under bounded resources, where active process units update iteratively using local neighborhoods, explicit energy/cost constraints, ternary decisions, optional memory fields, and halt conditions.

Natural Math is adjacent to:
- cellular automata;
- agent-based modeling;
- stigmergy;
- ant colony optimization;
- slime mold models;
- L-systems;
- diffusion-limited aggregation;
- reaction-diffusion;
- phase-field modeling;
- graph growth;
- fracture mechanics;
- path planning;
- dynamical systems.

Do not claim these are new. The distinct public project is the integrated stack:

> finite local growth + memory-as-boundary + ternary gating + MCVA/AMCVA readout + SERA efficiency accounting + provenance/HOLD runtime records.

### Natural Math process states

Natural Math uses:

- EXTEND
- SENSE
- RESTRICT

**EXTEND** means continue, grow, branch, reinforce, or commit resources.

**SENSE** means inspect, pause, gather evidence, thicken, reroute, explore, or adapt without final commitment.

**RESTRICT** means halt, archive, prune, become inert, mark failure, or form a boundary.

Important distinction:

> **SENSE is a process state. HOLD is a decision/runtime state.**

Do not collapse them completely.

Mapping:

- Natural Math EXTEND -> may generate MCVA-readable form.
- Natural Math SENSE -> may trigger MCVA Gate HOLD.
- Natural Math RESTRICT -> may generate scars, halt sites, boundaries, or AMCVA-relevant nonviability.

### Natural Math memory fields

Natural Math may use:

- trail memory;
- failure memory;
- success memory;
- pressure memory;
- erosion memory;
- depletion memory;
- archive memory;
- reinforcement memory.

Memory update form:

```text
M(p, t+1) = λ M(p, t) + d(p, t)
```

Where:

- `M` is local memory;
- `λ` is retention/decay;
- `d` is deposit from current activity.

This is the mechanism behind:

> Memory becomes geometry.

### Natural Math in the public repo

The v0.1-alpha public repo should include a simple Natural Math simulation:

- finite grid;
- seed-controlled;
- active site(s);
- obstacle field;
- finite energy/cost;
- EXTEND/SENSE/RESTRICT states;
- memory-enabled vs memory-disabled mode;
- trace output;
- JSON output;
- SERA metrics.

The simulation only needs to show that memory fields alter morphology and measurable efficiency. It does not need to prove any real-world domain.

## 11. MCVA and Natural Math together

Natural Math is the generator.
MCVA is the readout.

Natural Math asks:

> What local constrained process could have generated this morphology?

MCVA asks:

> What does the observed morphology measure, signal, or compare to?

Together they create a forward/inverse loop:

```text
Observed morphology
    -> MCVA descriptors
    -> MCVA/HOLD/AMCVA gate
    -> Natural Math candidate simulations
    -> observed-vs-generated morphology comparison
    -> process-hypothesis scores
    -> SERA cost/uncertainty accounting
    -> atlas update / HOLD / report
```

A public v0.1-alpha should not overbuild this. It should document the loop and implement small demos.

## 12. SERA

**SERA** means **Software Efficiency & Resilience Activation**.

In this project, SERA is the accounting layer for cost, waste, retries, false commitment, unresolved-state handling, drift, and valid-output efficiency.

SERA asks:

- Did memory reduce wasted exploration?
- Did HOLD prevent false commitment?
- Did descriptors reduce rework?
- Did the system route ambiguous cases correctly?
- What is cost per valid classification?
- What is cost per successful path?
- What is the review load?
- What is the efficiency delta between memory and no-memory?

Initial SERA metrics:

- hold_rate;
- coverage;
- selective_accuracy;
- false_positive_commitments_avoided;
- review_load_rate;
- cost_per_valid_classification;
- wasted_steps;
- repeated_failure_avoided;
- memory_efficiency_delta;
- semantic_rework_avoided_estimate.

For roads:

- cost per lane-mile processed;
- valid maintenance findings per inspection hour;
- repair-effectiveness morphology delta;
- review load per road segment;
- maintenance-priority stability.

For Natural Math:

- total steps;
- dead ends;
- repeated failures;
- success rate;
- cost per success;
- memory vs no-memory delta.

## 14. Construction A+

**Construction A+** is the future morphology-token/readout/substrate architecture.

Natural Math explains how candidate morphology may be generated by finite local constrained processes.

Construction A+ concerns how morphology may be read or tokenized as machine-native structure/instruction.

Keep these separate:

- Natural Math = how morphology can be generated.
- MCVA = how morphology is described/read.
- Construction A+ = how morphology may be physically/substrate-read or tokenized.

Public-safe statement:

> Construction A+ is a proposed morphology-token/readout architecture for representing physical or generated morphology as machine-readable tokens.

Do not claim it is fully implemented in the public MCVA v0.1-alpha unless code and validation exist.

## 16. T81 / Axion / HOLD

**T81/Axion** is the future deterministic runtime/provenance/gating architecture.

Public v0.1-alpha should not claim full T81 integration unless it exists.

It should define JSON records that could map to future T81/Axion.

Mapping:

- EXTEND -> Allow / proceed.
- SENSE -> Hold / inspect.
- RESTRICT -> Deny / halt / archive.

Decision-state distinctions:

- HOLD = unresolved evidence.
- Quarantine = unsafe/contaminated/security-relevant isolation.
- Deny/Reject = affirmative block.
- AMCVA = non-diagnostic/absent/misleading morphology; not necessarily unsafe.

## 29. Definition of “release-safe”

A file is release-safe if:

- it has no secrets;
- it has no private personal/legal/medical/financial material;
- it has no unlicensed third-party data;
- it has cautious public claims;
- it fits the open doctrine;
- it can be licensed under Apache-2.0 or CC BY 4.0;
- it is useful to the public release.

If uncertain, classify as NEEDS_OWNER_REVIEW.
