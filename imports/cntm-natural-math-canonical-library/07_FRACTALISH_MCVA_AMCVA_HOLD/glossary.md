# Glossary

- **MCVA**: Morphological Computation Vocabulary Atlas.
- **AMCVA**: Negative or guardrail atlas for absent, erased, hidden, misleading, or insufficient morphology.
- **HOLD**: Unresolved evidentiary state; do not force binary commitment.
- **Natural Math**: Forward or generative local-process simulation layer.
- **Morphological Memory Theory**: Thesis that morphology may preserve process evidence.
- **SERA**: Cost, waste, retry, false-commitment, and valid-output accounting.
- **Infinitysight**: Future machine-native perception layer.
- **Construction A+**: Future morphology-token and readout architecture.
- **SymLan**: Future symbolic compression layer after descriptor stabilization.
- **Atlas Manifest**: Metadata-first entry describing a candidate morphology example and its publication rights.
