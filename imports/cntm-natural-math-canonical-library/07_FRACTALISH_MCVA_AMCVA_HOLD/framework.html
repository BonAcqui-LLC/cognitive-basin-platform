Framework overview

The stack at a glance.

Fractalish is not one claim. It is a layered research stack for reading process from form, generating candidate morphologies under bounded local rules, instrumenting inward process fields, and preserving uncertainty where signal does not safely speak.

Fractalish

Fractalish is process forensics: the disciplined reading of process from form. It asks whether observable morphology preserves evidence of growth, damage, recovery, constraint, erasure, pressure, routing, or memory.

Natural Math

Natural Math is the local transition grammar: a bounded generative framework for finite updates under energy, pressure, trail memory, conflict, and termination constraints.

Cognitive Basin & Activation Runtime

Cognitive Basin is the instrumented process field. It treats reasoning paths, contradiction scars, trust channels, recovery routes, and HOLD fog as process morphology over time. The public boundary remains explicit: not-consciousness simulation, not a claim of subjective awareness.

MCVA / AMCVA / HOLD

MCVA is the vocabulary for where morphology may safely speak. AMCVA records where it does not safely speak. HOLD is the restraint against false closure when evidence, baseline, or segmentation is insufficient.

SymLan / Construction A+ / InfinitySight

These are the symbolic and perception-facing lanes: how stable symbolic structures, machine-native records, and future module languages might be earned from settled substrate structure rather than imposed too early.

Fractalish-Cognitive Basin integration

Cognitive Basin is Fractalish turned inward. The same attention to transition, recovery, constraint, and memory that applies to rivers, cracks, roots, or scars is being cautiously explored in transparent synthetic cognition traces.
