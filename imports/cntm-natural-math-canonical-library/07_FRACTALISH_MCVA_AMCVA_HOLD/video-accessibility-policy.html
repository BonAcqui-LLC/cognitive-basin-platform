Policy Note · Updated June 2026

No knowledge trapped in video

A video may introduce the idea. The text must carry the idea. The trace must test the idea. Fractalish uses this rule to keep knowledge public, inspectable, and accessible.

For Fractalish-produced video

  • Captions
  • Transcript
  • Chapter markers
  • Companion text page
  • Key claims summary
  • References
  • Downloadable study notes

For third-party video

  • Embed the official video.
  • Provide accessible timed subtitles when available.
  • Write original Fractalish study notes.
  • Attribute the source clearly.
  • Do not present third-party speech as Fractalish-authored content.
No knowledge trapped in video.
