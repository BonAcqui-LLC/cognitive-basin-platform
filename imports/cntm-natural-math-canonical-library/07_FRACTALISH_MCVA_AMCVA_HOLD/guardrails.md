# Fractalish Guardrails

Fractalish does not claim that every natural pattern is fractal. Nature uses many geometries. Fractalish studies when morphology preserves process information, and AMCVA marks when that information is absent, hidden, erased, non-diagnostic, dominated by another geometry, or not yet readable.

## Core guardrails

- Similarity is not identity.
- A fractal reading is not forced. It must be earned.
- Nature does not speak only in fractals.
- Fractalish must read the whole geometric field.
- MCVA reads preserved process. AMCVA prevents hallucinated process.
- AMCVA is not a failure state. It is how the system refuses false geometry.
- Every claim must travel with its trace.
- Single images classify; sequences explain.
- Repeated images beat perfect images.

## Safety boundary

- Do not infer temporal process from a single image without validated domain baseline.
- Do not make medical, structural safety, disaster prediction, or diagnostic claims.
- Include proof-negative and AMCVA examples.
- Preserve source and license metadata.
- Never silently overwrite submitted records.

## Geometry interactions

A circle can constrain a branch. A lattice can guide a dendrite. A wave can carry a fracture. The question is not which geometry wins. The question is what the interaction records.
