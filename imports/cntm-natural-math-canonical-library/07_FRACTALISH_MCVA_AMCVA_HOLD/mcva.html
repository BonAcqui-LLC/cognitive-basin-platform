MCVA

Read form carefully. Preserve uncertainty.

MCVA is the readout layer for morphology-bearing process evidence.

MCVA studies where observable morphology preserves enough organization to support descriptor extraction, baseline comparison, and process-memory hypotheses. AMCVA maps the negative or non-diagnostic boundary. HOLD preserves unresolved cases instead of forcing binary commitment.

A current research lane is the post-bifurcation recovery signature: the idea that the downstream recovery zone after a split may preserve more useful information than the split alone.

A split tells us the system encountered choice or constraint. The recovery zone tells us what the system became afterward.
