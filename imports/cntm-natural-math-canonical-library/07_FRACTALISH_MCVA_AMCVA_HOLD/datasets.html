Datasets

Public-data lanes, not hidden corpora.

Fractalish should prefer public, licensable, inspectable data.

The first application lane remains roads and public works because it is visually obvious, low regulatory risk, and compatible with manifest-first publication.

The recovery wake hypothesis fits this lane well: pavement morphology may preserve evidence of split propagation around voids, patches, aggregate boundaries, and water-channel damage, followed by downstream preferred routing.

Do not publish third-party assets until rights are verified. Where rights remain unresolved, publish a manifest entry and source link instead of the asset itself.
