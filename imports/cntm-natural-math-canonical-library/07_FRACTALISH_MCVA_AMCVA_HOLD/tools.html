Why the tooling matters

Fractalish is text-first and trace-first. The tools keep the trace tethered to the public claims, which is how critique can focus on the evidence instead of chasing rhetoric.

Cross-domain comparison framing

Cross-domain resemblance is not victory. It is a test: do the descriptor profile, topology, and process hypothesis travel once the trace is made explicit?

The text carries the idea. The trace tests the idea.
