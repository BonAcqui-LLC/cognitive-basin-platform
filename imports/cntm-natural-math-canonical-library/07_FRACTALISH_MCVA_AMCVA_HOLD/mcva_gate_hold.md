# MCVA Gate and HOLD

MCVA Gate is the ternary decision layer:

`MCVA / HOLD / AMCVA`

## HOLD

HOLD is a first-class unresolved evidentiary state.

HOLD means the system does not yet have enough support to commit to a positive or negative morphology decision. HOLD is not failure. It is the refusal to force a premature commit.

HOLD should be used when:

- confidence is low,
- evidence conflicts,
- resolution is poor,
- segmentation is weak,
- baseline coverage is inadequate,
- or multiple process explanations remain plausible.

## Distinctions

- `SENSE` belongs to the Natural Math process layer.
- `HOLD` belongs to the MCVA decision or runtime layer.
- `AMCVA` means the morphology is absent, misleading, or non-diagnostic.

HOLD is the anti-drift state.
